\section*{Preface}
This is the author's algebra notes, the content mainly contains Abstract Algebra and Commutative Algebra. Since this is a product of the author's fairly early use of Latex for typesetting, many of the contents (e.g., formulae cited, references, etc.) are missing, so I hope the reader will bear with me.\par
This material contains two parts: abstract algebra and commutative algebra. The main reference for abstract algebra is Hungerford: \textit{Algebra} (GTM73), and the main reference for commutative algebra is Atiyah and MacDonald: \textit{Commutative Algebra}. The author has also referred to other references, which will be listed in the main text.\par
Please note that this is not a formal lecture or book, but only as a personal note, so the main audience of this note is the author himself, some parts may be too jumpy, and other parts may be very wordy. In addition, due to the author's limited level, there are inevitable mistakes or errors in the text, and I urge readers to criticise and correct them.
